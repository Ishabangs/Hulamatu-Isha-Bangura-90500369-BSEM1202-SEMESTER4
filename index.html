As someone who is passionate about both beauty and technology, web design plays an important role in my growth. It also opens opportunities for me to work independently, support other small businesses, and even offer digital services alongside my hairstyling career.

Overall, I study web design to combine creativity with technology, grow my business, and prepare myself for a successful future in both the beauty and digital world.
